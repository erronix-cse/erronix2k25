Ballistic Bash

Ballistic Bash is an energetic and action-packed event designed to test your reflexes, precision, and strategy. Participants compete in fun and competitive challenges that involve aiming, launching, and striking targets with accuracy under time pressure. This event guarantees high intensity, excitement, and loads of entertainment for both participants and the audience.
