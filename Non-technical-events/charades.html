Charades — Dumb C

About the Event

Dumb C (Charades) is a fun and interactive event where participants test their creativity, quick thinking, and team coordination. One person acts without speaking, while the others guess the word, phrase, or title within a set time limit. It’s all about teamwork, gestures, and having fun!

Event Flow & Rules

  • Form Teams: Players are divided into teams.
  • Choose Roles: Each team selects one actor and at least one guesser.
  • Get the Item: A judge or organizer provides the actor with an item to mime (e.g., a movie title, actor, song, or phrase).
  • Mime and Guess: The actor begins miming the item without speaking, while teammates try to guess.
  • Set a Time Limit: Teams usually get 1 minute 30 seconds to guess correctly.
  • Win or Lose: The team that guesses within the time limit scores points.

Key Rules to Remember

  • No Talking: Actors cannot use words or sounds.
  • Use Gestures: Only hand/body movements are allowed.
  • Guessing is Allowed: Teammates may call out answers freely.
  • No Sign Language: Using alphabets or sign language is not permitted.

General Guidelines

  • Participants must register individually or as part of a team.
  • Follow the instructions provided by the event coordinators.
  • Cheating or unfair play leads to disqualification.
  • Decisions by judges are final and binding.
  • Maintain sportsmanship and respect for fellow participants.
