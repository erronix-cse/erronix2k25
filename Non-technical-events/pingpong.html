Ping-Pong-Throw

About the Event

Ping-Pong-Throw is a fun-filled, skill-testing event where participants showcase their accuracy, speed, and coordination. Compete against others in a series of challenges that test your precision in throwing ping-pong balls into designated targets. This event is designed to be thrilling and entertaining for both players and spectators.

Game Rules & Flow

  • Form Players/Teams: Players can play in teams of 3 to 5 members. Each team gets equal chances.
  • Get the Balls: Each player is given 3–5 ping pong balls per turn.
  • Throw and Aim: Players stand behind the throwing line and throw balls one by one, aiming to land them inside cups/bowls filled with water.
  • Set a Time Limit (Optional): A 1-minute limit per turn can be added for excitement. Players must finish throws within the time.
  • Win or Lose: Successful throws (ball lands & stays inside) are counted. The team with the most points or successful throws wins.

Key Rules to Remember

  • No Crossing the Line: Players must always throw from behind the line.
  • Use Only Provided Balls: No extra balls are allowed.
  • One Throw at a Time: Players cannot throw multiple balls together.
  • Bounces Don’t Count: Unless stated, balls must directly land & stay inside the cup/bowl.

General Guidelines

  • Respect the coordinators and follow all given instructions.
  • No cheating, tampering, or interference allowed.
  • Maintain fair play and sportsmanship throughout the event.
  • Judges’ and coordinators’ decisions are final.
