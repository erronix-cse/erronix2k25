Rhythm Riddle

About the Event

Rhythm Riddle is an exciting non-technical event where participants solve musical and rhythmic puzzles. Test your timing, logic, and problem-solving skills while keeping the beat in this engaging challenge.

Teams & Format

  • Each team may have a maximum of 3 members.
  • The contest consists of three rounds.

Rounds

  1. Round 1 – Speaker Box: Teams listen to short soundtrack clips and identify the song.
  2. Round 2 – Bioscope: Teams identify songs from images shown on screen.
  3. Round 3 – Guess the Missing Words: Teams identify the missing word(s) from a displayed lyric.

Time Rules

  • Each round lasts a maximum of 15 minutes.
  • Each round consists of 5 songs/questions.
  • For each song/question, the time limit to answer is 2 minutes.

How to Answer

  • Teams must press the horn/buzzer before answering.
  • Only the team that buzzes may answer that item.
  • If a team gives a wrong answer, they lose further chances for the remaining items in that round.

Result & Prize

  • A total of 15 songs will be played in the contest.
  • The top three teams will be selected and awarded prizes.
  • Final decision will be based on points scored.

Final Notes

  • Organizers’ decisions are final.
  • Play fair and respect the time limits.
