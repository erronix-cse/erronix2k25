Registration Protocol

  • Read event rules carefully and check timings before registering.
  • Register in advance and confirm your attendance.
  • Be punctual for all sessions and events.
  • Maintain respectful conduct with coordinators, speakers, and participants.
  • Adhere to your allotted time if presenting or performing.
  • Follow the professional dress code.
  • Carry and display your college ID card at all times.
  • Follow instructions from event coordinators and volunteers.
  • Avoid unnecessary phone calls; use official email for queries.
