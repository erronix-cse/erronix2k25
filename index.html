Registration Protocol

  • Registration: Ensure you register in advance and confirm your attendance.
  • Respectful Engagement: Engage respectfully with speakers and fellow participants. Allow for questions and discussions.
  • Adherence to Time Limits: If presenting, stick to your allotted time to allow for a smooth schedule.
  • Dress Code: Follow any specified dress code to maintain professionalism.
  • ID Card: Participants must wear their College ID Card. Be on time for all sessions, including keynote speeches and panel discussions.
