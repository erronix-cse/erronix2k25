Mindclash – Technical Quiz

About the Event

Mindclash is a technical quiz designed to test knowledge, analytical skills, and quick thinking of Computer Science enthusiasts. The event consists of multiple rounds with increasing difficulty, covering core computer science topics and emerging technologies.

Category & Participation

  • Category/Theme: Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

Registration & Prerequisites

  • Registration Process: Online
  • Prerequisite: Registration completed prior to the event
  • Participants must arrive at the venue at least 10 minutes before the start time

Event Overview

  • The quiz is divided into three rounds with increasing difficulty.
  • Participants are expected to manage their time effectively and answer accurately.

General Rules & Guidelines

DOs:

  1. Arrive at the quiz venue before the scheduled time.
  2. Carefully read and understand the quiz rules and format.
  3. Manage your time effectively during the quiz.
  4. Bring your own pens and necessary materials.

DON’Ts:

  1. No cheating or malpractice will be tolerated.
  2. Do not discuss questions or answers with other participants.
  3. Electronic devices (smartphones, smartwatches, etc.) are strictly prohibited.
  4. Avoid any behavior that could be considered unfair or against the rules.
  5. Respect the judges’ and officials’ decisions; disputes are not allowed.

Event Flow & Time Allocation

  • Round 1 – Elimination: 20 Easy Questions (Topics: Machine Learning, OOPS, Data Structures, Cybersecurity)
  • Round 2 – Elimination: 15 Medium Questions (Topics: Operating Systems, DBMS, Computer Networks, Algorithms)
  • Round 3 – Winner Selection: 10 Hard Questions (Topics: Coding Fundamentals, Web Development, Data Structures & Algorithms)

Evaluation Criteria

  • Correctness of answers
  • Accuracy in coding/problem-solving (Round 3)

Code of Conduct

  • Participants must follow all rules strictly.
  • Misbehaviour, disputes, or violations may lead to disqualification.
  • Maintain professionalism and decorum during the quiz.
