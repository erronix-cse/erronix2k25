Creathon — Project Expo

About the Event

Creathon is a competitive project exhibition where participants showcase innovative ideas and working prototypes in computer science and technology. It provides a platform for students to demonstrate creativity, technical expertise, and problem-solving skills to a panel of judges and an enthusiastic audience.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • Projects must be original and developed by the team.
  • All project code and documentation should be submitted before the deadline.
  • Participants should be prepared for a live demonstration and Q&A session.
  • Judges’ decisions are final.
