Paper Presentation

The Paper Presentation event encourages students to research, innovate, and present their ideas in front of a panel of experts. Participants can showcase their technical knowledge, research skills, and creativity.

Rules of the Event:

  • Participants must register in teams of 1-3 members.
  • Each presentation must be within 10 minutes, followed by 5 minutes Q&A.
  • Original work only; plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • Use of multimedia aids is allowed.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.
