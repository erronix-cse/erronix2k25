Epic Frame — Wireframing

About the Event

Epic Frame is a creative wireframing competition where participants design user interfaces for real-world problems. The event emphasizes clarity, usability, and innovation in design, testing participants’ ability to convert abstract ideas into effective digital blueprints.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams can have a maximum of 2 members.
  • Wireframes must be original and created during the event.
  • Participants may use any wireframing tool (Figma, Adobe XD, Balsamiq, etc.).
  • Final submissions should include both low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes.
  • Designs will be judged on creativity, usability, and presentation clarity.
  • Judges’ decisions are final.
