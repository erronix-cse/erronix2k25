About the Event

Rhythm Riddle is an exciting non-technical event where participants solve musical and rhythmic puzzles. Test your timing, logic, and problem-solving skills while keeping the beat in this engaging challenge.

Rules of the Event

  • Each participant must register individually.
  • Participants will be given a series of rhythm-based challenges.
  • Use of external instruments is allowed if specified in each round.
  • Decisions of the judges are final and binding.
  • Participants must maintain decorum throughout the event.
