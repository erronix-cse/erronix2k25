Charades-Dumb C

About the Event: Dumb C is a fun and challenging gaming event that tests your reflexes, strategy, and quick thinking. Compete with fellow participants and show your skills in a thrilling experience.

Rules of the Event:

  • Participants must register individually or in teams as specified.
  • Follow the instructions provided by the event coordinators during gameplay.
  • Any form of cheating or unfair play will lead to disqualification.
  • Decisions of the judges are final and binding.
  • Respect other participants and maintain sportsmanship throughout the event.
